Team leasing

The perfect solution for you when
  • you need specific and proven engeeniers
  • you do not want to create an extensive internal IT team
  • you want a fast project implementation without delays caused by lack of resources

Managed Team

You should choose when
  • you want to focus all your resources on your company’s main objectives
  • you want to move responsibility to the IT vendor
  • you do not want to build a dedicated team responsible for a given project/area
  • you want to optimise costs while maintaining high quality and availability of services

Offshore/Nearshore Delivery centers

Decide on this solution when
  • you want to build professional IT engineering teams servicing your entire organization
  • you want to develop several engineering teams as your business needs grow
  • you don’t want to waste time and resources looking for qualified people to join your team
  • you want the best results while maintaining a high quality-to-price ratio
  • confidentiality and data security are important to you

How do we work with IT projects?

When working with our clients, we always focus on complete service, long-term partnerships and ensuring the highest standards of work by developers. This is why our clients can count on the support of a Customer Success Manager, Site Manager and HR Business Partner.

Customer Success Manager

  • Verification of all IT Resources and Development needs
  • Onboarding and building relationships with Engineers and Customers
  • Responsible for Client – Centric approach and attrition level

Site Manager

  • Managing projects portfolio
  • Cooperating with Customer Technical Managers
  • Overseeing projects delivery
  • Keeping SLAs/KPIs
  • First point of contact and escalations

HR Business Partner

  • Supporting HR matters: people development, motivation, feedback, talent development
  • Onboarding and implementation processes
  • Driving and supporting company culture and values
  • Sharing best practices and experience through proactive self-development

Scaling up / scaling down

Prime vendor

Engineers’ mobility

You have the opportunity to actually get to know the engineers you work with. Engineers enjoy participating in business trips to clients abroad.

Tech department

The team working for you has regular contact with technology leaders and IT directors. As a result, they know how to solve the most difficult tasks.

Increasing competences

We offer complimentary training for dedicated employees, because we believe in investing in our team and delivering exceptional value to our clients. As a result, we provide services of the highest quality.

Low turnover

We were awarded the best IT workplace in Poland 5 times. We build lasting relationships with our people and create conditions for their growth. As a result, our team is made up of long-term employees.

“Buddy cooperation”

The mid-developer working for you always has the support of a senior specialist who ensures the quality and outcome of the IT project.

Anti-lock agreement

We play fair. We do not make you dependent. If you decide to stop working with us, you can always do so, and if necessary, we will facilitate data transfer.

In-house compliance department

Our compliance department ensures that the developers working for you comply with both external and internal regulations. It also identifies risks and gives advice on how to avoid or remedy them. It guards data security, among other things, in accordance with current GDPR regulations.

High quality and cost optimisation

Nearshoring to Poland guarantees good prices and goes hand in hand with the high quality of the services offered. We have an impressive pool of talented programmers in our team. They help in improving efficiency and saving time for in-house employees and help deliver projects faster.

Knowledge Harvest

We have built an internal process where we capture and record the tacit knowledge and experience of IT team members to help others adapt and apply it. This enables us to manage changes and minimise the risk of errors. At the same time, we increase the efficiency of project work at clients.

Allocation Center

A dedicated team is responsible for the efficient management of our specialists' work, according to their competencies and client requirements. Your project will be handled by experienced IT engineers. You will not see any disruption or lack of resources in your project.
